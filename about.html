About Bibliohead Bookstore

Founded in 2004, Bibliohead Bookstore is a gem of a small store in the heart of San Francisco. Helpful and friendly, we carry an eclectic assortment from select bestsellers to rare, oddball and hard to find.

We buy books for trade and cash. We're especially looking for super current and classic literature, and books on all the arts from painting to dance and poetry.

Please call ahead if you want to bring books in for sale: (415) 621-6772.

Our Hours:

  • Mon-Sat 11-9
  • Sun 12-8

Don't forget to like us on Facebook and visit our News section for updates!

Special areas of interest include:

Music and Dance, especially classical and opera-we're right down by the San Francisco Symphony, Opera and Ballet, and the new home of the Conservatory of Music. Western and Eastern Philosophy, Old Metaphysics, Occult and Magic.

Come visit. You'll be glad you did.
